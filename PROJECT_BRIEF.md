# PROJECT_BRIEF.md

## Project name

zerobaker

## Owner

王珺怡 / Evelyn Wang

## Project concept

zerobaker is Wang Junyi's personal website and an online-only bakery.

The website uses baking as a narrative metaphor:

* completed projects and achievements are freshly baked works;
* experiences and skills are ingredients accumulated over time;
* interests, music, travel, fandom, and everyday stories are secret recipes;
* the website itself is her first vibe-coding project created through learning and exploring AI.

The site should present the owner as a complete person rather than functioning only as a recruitment landing page.

## Primary goals

1. Present Wang Junyi's education, work, content-creation, activity-planning, and AI exploration experiences.
2. Display selected videos, operational projects, offline activities, and future AI products.
3. Express her personal aesthetics, interests, observations, and stories.
4. Serve as a reusable personal homepage and portfolio.
5. Demonstrate her ability to complete a website through vibe coding.

## Audience

* recruiters and interviewers;
* potential collaborators;
* social-media followers;
* friends and people interested in her work;
* the owner herself in the future.

## Page structure

The website is a seamless single-page site with four main sections:

1. 自我介绍 / About the Baker
2. 作品展示 / Freshly Baked
3. 烘焙秘方 / Secret Recipes
4. 联系方式 / Find Me

A transparent navigation bar remains visible at the top and allows users to jump to each section.

## Homepage

The homepage contains:

* handwritten zerobaker logo;
* transparent navigation;
* central welcome text;
* a dotted path spelling LAYOVER;
* a cut-out photo taken in Jeju Island;
* four illustrated cake slices that link to the four main sections.

Homepage copy:

“欢迎你！我是王珺怡”

“这是我的个人网站，也是一家只在网上营业的烘焙坊。戳戳小蛋糕，可以了解更多信息哦～”

## About section

Use one main personal photo and three narrative parts:

1. Learning experience
2. Work experience
3. Other experiences, fandom, AI exploration, and vibe coding

This section should tell a personal story rather than repeat a résumé.

## Projects section

Display video, content-operation, event, and AI projects.

Each project should contain:

* cover image;
* title;
* category or platform;
* short description;
* result or data;
* personal role;
* external link.

The project list must be easy to update through `content.js`.

## Secret Recipes section

Display approximately seven interactive paper balls.

Each paper ball opens into a paper note containing:

* one image;
* one paragraph;
* optional audio, video, or external link.

Possible topics include:

* BTS;
* a recommended song;
* a recent film or series;
* a current interest;
* a travel memory;
* an everyday moment;
* a personal story.

Desktop may support mouse repulsion and rolling. Mobile must use a simpler tap interaction.

## Contact section

Use a split layout:

* left: social-media links and email;
* right: a dessert photograph taken by the owner.

Include Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Bilibili, and email.

The final handwritten decorative line is:

“Maybe we could be slow dancing”

## Visual direction

* warm textured paper background across the entire website;
* hand-drawn bakery stickers in selected corners;
* collage, paper, and handmade visual details;
* clear readable body text;
* handwritten type used only for titles and decorative details;
* warm and playful, but not childish or visually crowded.
